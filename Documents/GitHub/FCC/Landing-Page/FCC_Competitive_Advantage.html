Why Choose FCC

  1. AI Command Layer: FCC doesn’t just display data — it acts on it. Through Claude or ChatGPT copilots, users can automate forecasting, detect anomalies, or generate reports instantly.
  2. Data Sovereignty: FCC is built for finance teams who can’t compromise on privacy — all credentials and transactions remain fully under client control.
  3. Unified Compliance: Instead of relying on third-party SaaS compliance, FCC integrates its own MCP audit layer — scanning Plaid, Stripe, and Xero transactions for risk and policy violations in real time.
  4. Extensible Ecosystem: Open API architecture lets clients plug in QuickBooks, ERP, payroll, or banking APIs — making FCC a true command layer over all financial systems.
  5. Future-Proofed AI Infrastructure: FCC’s modular AI stack supports Claude, OpenAI, or Gemini models — ensuring long-term adaptability to any LLM ecosystem.
